Principal Investigator

Dr. Rock Mancini

Principal Investigator

Bio

Rock was born and raised in Hampton, a sleepy suburb in the North Hills of Pittsburgh Pennsylvania. He received his B.S. degree in Chemistry from the University of Pittsburgh where he performed research with Toby Chapman. From there, he ventured to Los Angeles in search of warmer climates. He received his Ph.D. in Organic Chemistry researching as a NSF-IGERT fellow in the lab of Heather Maynard at UCLA followed by postdoctoral work at the University of California, Irvine with Aaron Esser-Kahn. After a short stint launching his independent career at Washington State University, Rock is currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemistry & Biochemistry at Miami University.
